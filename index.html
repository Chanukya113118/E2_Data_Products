About

"Everything is data, and Everywhere is data" refers to the idea that data is pervasive and is found in many different contexts.

Another aspect of the statement "everything is data, and everywhere is data" is the idea that data is increasingly being collected and generated from a wide variety of sources and in many different contexts.

Overall, the idea that "everything is data, and everywhere is data" highlights the central role that data plays in modern society, and the importance of being able to visualize, analyze, and predict data in order to make informed decisions.
